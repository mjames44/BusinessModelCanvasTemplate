The Business Model Canvas

Key Partners

The most importantly partner our project depends on, at this time, is WordPress. For that reason, it is important to us to always stay current with the most popular engines driving web design and development.

Key Activities

The key activities of our project will be keeping up with the current needs of our customers to proceed the rich, robust, full featured WordPress sites they need, without the burden of falling behind their competition.

Also, keeping our training current is vitally important.

Value Proposition

Our product provides a new, convenient, highly usable interface for the creation and customization of WordPress servers.

Our customers will experience faster set up, greater savings, without the usual barriers to entry.

The results will be a greater opportunity for our users to focus on their content, their customers, and their design, instead of learning fast changing technologies.

Customer Relationship

Our project aims to create strong personal relationships with our customers through personal assistance, self-service training, and by creating a community of users who can serve as a resource for new ideas and uses for our product.

Customer Segments

Our project will use a diversified model to focus on small to medium business owners and content creators .

Cost Structure

The final cost structure of our project will require greater research, but at this point it is clear that initial production, maintenance, support, marketing, and developing customer training will be major elements therein.

Revenue Streams

Our revenue streams will consist of three sources:

First, the initial sale of the product.

Second, premium subscriptions to advanced training video courses.

Third, from extended support beyond quick phone call, chat, or email support.

Costs will included support, distribution, and maintenance expenses; as well as product marketing and production costs for training videos.
